06 — Creative Strategy

Priority Playbook

01

Fabric Storytelling

Close-up texture clips, why-it's-premium messaging, and care instructions — converting skeptics into buyers by making the quality tangible.

02

Fit & Size Content

Short fit explainer videos paired with clear, visible size charts and a transparent return policy to eliminate hesitation at the decision point.

03

Styling Breakdowns

3–5 outfit ideas per SKU to increase average order value and drive cross-category discovery between trousers, blazers, and skirts.

04

Value Positioning

"Why this blazer is different" — benefit-led messaging that goes beyond aesthetics into pricing justification and material differentiation.

07 — Ad Structure

Tactical Fixes

01

Prospecting — Broad Interest

Test creative concepts and audiences with low-budget experiments. Lean on content collaborations to seed top-of-funnel awareness efficiently.

02

Retargeting — Website & IG Viewers

SKU-specific ads with product clips and fit proof served to warm audiences who have already engaged with the brand organically.

03

Scale — Allocate to Winners

Increase budget only on proven SKUs, validated creatives, and confirmed available fabric. No scaling ahead of supply certainty.
